CarSavior

"CarSavior is a suite of tools for the NG Infotainment System that allow for you, your family, and your car to stay safe, and for this project specifically in the city of Detroit. Detroit is a city with a high crime rate and in some areas it can be very safe to travel through, let alone park in or walk through."

  • Safety: Utilized Detroit Crime API to analyze and report nearby crime rates 
  • Alerts: Notify owner of vehicle when teen has been in an accident 
  • Mechanical: Utilize GM’s API to detect mechanical issues and provide fixes
