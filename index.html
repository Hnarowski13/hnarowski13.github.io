Start Up

Kudo LLC

Cofounder and CTO

Kudo is a social networking productivity app focused on self improvement and community based habit building.

We all have goals we want to achieve, habits we want to build, actions we want to be doing to become better versions of ourselves everyday. The challenge is staying on track, being consistent, staying motivated, and reaching these goals in a systematic way. It can be so discouraging to rely on willpower and grit alone to form habits or accomplish goals. To remedy this problem and help people overcome that challenge, we built a tool that makes achieving goals easier, more fun, and more meaningful.

Kudo is an application that allows groups of people to achieve goals together. An incredible thing happens when people share their goals and habit building with their friends and family. What previously felt like a chore that needed to be completed and checked off becomes an engaging, social, and interactive experience.
